# Important Reminders

These are clarifications for commonly confused rules.

## Round 9 and Beyond
If the game reaches round 9 (after multiple drawn rounds), players continue drawing cards from the **level IV deck only** - NOT level V. The round marker stays on round 8 visually, but the game continues with level IV cards until someone wins 4 rounds.

## Card Levels and Decks
- Level I cards: Rounds 1-2
- Level II cards: Rounds 3-4
- Level III cards: Rounds 5-6
- Level IV cards: Rounds 7-8 (and round 9+)
- Level V cards: Only obtained through special card abilities, never drawn during normal draft phase
