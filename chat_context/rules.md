# Amfibido --- A Dojo Duel

## Introduction

Amfibido is a tactical head‑to‑head card game where players draft
fighters and battle across three dojos. First player to win **4 rounds**
becomes the **Grand Sensei**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Components

### Level Decks

-   32 Level I cards
-   32 Level II cards
-   31 Level III cards
-   26 Level IV cards
-   10 Level V cards

### Other

-   15 Sensei cards
-   8 Dojo cards
-   Score board
-   Player tokens
-   Round marker

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Setup

1.  Shuffle the five **level decks (I--V)** separately.
2.  Each player places **three dojos** and a **cooldown zone**.
3.  Place the **score board** and set the **round marker to 1**.
4.  Place both player tokens on **0**.
5.  Randomly choose a starting player.
6.  Each player draws **3 cards from Level I**.

If a player has **no fighters**, they may reshuffle and draw again.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Gameplay Overview

Each round contains four phases:

1.  Draft Phase
2.  Action Phase
3.  Fight Phase
4.  End of Round

The player who wins the **most dojo fights** wins the round.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Draft Phase

Players:

1.  Draw **3 cards**
2.  Keep **1**
3.  Pass **2 cards to opponent**
4.  Choose **1 from received cards**
5.  Discard the last card

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Action Phase

Players alternate actions:

-   Play fighter
-   Play equipment
-   Play action card
-   Activate ability
-   Pass

When both players pass → **Fight Phase begins**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Fight Phase

Fight outcomes:

-   **No fighters** → Draw
-   **One fighter** → Automatic win
-   **Two fighters** → Compare power

Total Power = Base Power + Equipment + Abilities + Combo bonuses

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## End of Round

Steps:

1.  Return cooldown cards to hand
2.  Discard combo moves
3.  Return defeated fighters
4.  Advance round marker

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Keywords

**Boomerang** -- Goes to cooldown instead of discard.

**Knockout** -- Fighter goes to cooldown and skips next fight.

**Switcheroo** -- Swap fighters or equipment between dojos.

**Neutralize** -- Ignore card abilities.
